App Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 January 2026

1. Who we are

SoftOcean Ltd, 57 Thornhill Road, Plymouth, Devon, PL3 5NF. Data controller for the PlanktoSpace Service.

2. Information Collection

A. Device Identifiers

A "deviceUid" is sent to our backend for operational logging, troubleshooting, and security. We do not use this for behavioral profiling or advertising.

B. Crash Reports (Sentry)

We use Sentry to fix bugs and improve reliability. Reports include technical info like device model and OS version, but exclude user-inputted content.

C. Location Information

The App uses location data to help you find satellite overpasses. This is processed in transit; we do not store coordinates or location history on our servers.

3. Legal Bases

We rely on Performance of a Contract, Legitimate Interests (securing and improving the Service), and Consent (for precise location access).

4. Contact

For privacy questions: softoceanltd@gmail.com

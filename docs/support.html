Support & Contact

How to reach us

Email is the most efficient way to resolve your queries. Our team aims to respond within a reasonable time, prioritizing critical stability issues.

softoceanltd@gmail.com

What to include in your request

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

  • A brief description of the problem.
  • Device type and Operating System version.
  • App version number.
  • Any error codes or request IDs shown in the App.

Privacy & Feedback

For data requests, please use the subject line "Privacy Request". For suggestions and ideas, use "Feature Request".

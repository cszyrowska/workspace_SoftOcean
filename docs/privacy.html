Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 January 2026

1. Who we are

SoftOcean Ltd is the data controller for personal information described in this Policy. Based in Plymouth, Devon (Company No. 09273776), we are committed to absolute transparency regarding your data.

2. Information Collection & Use

A. Operational Identifiers

We use a unique device identifier (deviceUid) to monitor backend performance and ensure security. This data is never used for behavioral profiling or advertising.

B. Crash Reporting (Sentry)

To ensure Plaspa remains stable, we use Sentry to collect diagnostic data. This includes device models and OS versions, but excludes any content you input into the App.

C. Location Data

The App uses GPS to assist in finding satellite overpasses. We process this data in transit only; we do not store your coordinates or location history on our servers.

3. Legal Compliance

We operate under UK GDPR and EU GDPR frameworks. Our processing is based on the performance of our contract with you, our legitimate interest in maintaining a secure service, and your explicit consent for location access.
